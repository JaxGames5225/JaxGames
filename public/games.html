Runner

Download

Runner is a fun to play mini game that progressively gets harder as you go on. Start off as a highschool sophmore and see if you can make it to the olympics while collecting as many gold medals as possable!

Mini Golf

Download

Mini Golf is a fun simple round of par 3 mini golf. Play all nine holes and see if you can get better the more you play. Avoid the sand pits and water hazards and get to the hole. Have fun, and putt on!

Targets

Download

Targets is a simple yet addicting game. You play as a person shooting at targets. Each time you hit the target it gets launched back into the air. The more times you hit it before it hits the ground the higher your multiplier becomes. See if you can beat my highscore!

Football

Download

Football is a small game that progressively gets harder as you go on. You play as a kick returner and have to make your way to the endzone in order to complete each level. You must choose which plays to call and where to run in order to get past the opposing team. Good Luck!

Jacks Game of Time

Play Now

Jacks Game of Time is the most advanced of all of my games. It is a 3D platformer with a big twist. You play as a time controlling block that has the ability to freeze and reverse time. The combination of strategy and platforming makes Jacks Game of time the hardest Game of them all!

Short Fuse

Donate to Help

Short fuse is an online multiplayer first person shooter. But unlike all others you play as a bomb. Your fuse is constantly burning, but each time you land a shot on another player your fuse grows. When your fuse goes out you explode killing players in your radius. The more kills you have the larger your explosion. Fight to claim a spot on your leaderboard!
