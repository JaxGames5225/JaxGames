Stretchy-Man

View Store Page

Stretchy-Man is a physics based puzzle game. The famous superhero Super-Duper-Man has been captured by the villain Evil-Man, leaving it up to the unlikely hero Stretchy-Man to save the world from destruction. Will you be able to stop Evil-Man before it's too late?

Jacks Game of Time

Play Now

Jacks Game of Time is a 3D platformer with a big twist. You play as a time controlling block that has the ability to freeze and reverse time. The combination of strategy and platforming makes Jacks Game of time the hardest Game of them all!

Mouse Snake

Visit Website

Mouse Snake is a fun maze game. Place your mouse in the circle to begin. The faster you move the mouse, the less you can see, but be careful not to hit a wall or yourself! Collect multipliers to get more points, and try to set a new high score!

Learning Rockets

View on GitHub

Learning Rockets is a project that shows the process of a genetic algorithm. The goal of the program is for the rockets to learn how to navigate the asteroid field using a genetic algorithm. Statistics such as the mutation rate, population size, and generation are displayed at the bottom

Electric Field Visualizer

View on GitHub

This program is meant to show the effects of positive and negative charges on an electric field. It allows you to place positive and negative charges of different sizes, as well as drop test charges to see the motion of a particle in the field

Neural Network Car

Visit Website

This program is meant to display the inner workings of a neural network. The program allows the user to design and train their own neural network. Once they are confident in their neural network, they can test their car on a track never before seen by the neural network.

Football

Download

Football is a small game that progressively gets harder as you go on. You play as a kick returner and have to make your way to the endzone in order to complete each level. You must choose which plays to call and where to run in order to get past the opposing team. Good Luck!

Targets

Download

Targets is a simple yet addicting game. You play as a person shooting at targets. Each time you hit the target it gets launched back into the air. The more times you hit it before it hits the ground the higher your multiplier becomes. See if you can beat my highscore!

Mini Golf

Download

Mini Golf is a fun simple round of par 3 mini golf. Play all nine holes and see if you can get better the more you play. Avoid the sand pits and water hazards and get to the hole. Have fun, and putt on!

Runner

Download

Runner is a fun to play mini game that progressively gets harder as you go on. Start off as a highschool sophmore and see if you can make it to the olympics while collecting as many gold medals as possible!
